What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is calculated based on a ratio of your weight and height and is used as a general guideline to determine possible health risks.

If you've heard of BMI before, you may have also heard that it's not accurate for individual people because it was created to evaluate the risk of the general population. There is come truth to this, however, determining your BMI is a good place to start when deciding if you need to lose weight.
